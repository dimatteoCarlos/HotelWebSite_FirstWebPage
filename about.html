About Hotel Coco Island

There is nothing like a getaway to a little known island paradise. The Cocos (Keeling) Islands is just that for your next tropical holiday escape.

There are several styles of accommodation specifically designed to suit your vacation requirements and budget.

With steady trade winds, crystal clear water and beautiful beaches, Cocos Island is a world-renown spot for kite surfing. If you're up for an adventure, give it a try.
